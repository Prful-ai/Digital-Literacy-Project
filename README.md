#  Task 1: Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic

##  Overview

The Digital Literacy project is doing a task. This task is about making a one-page infographic. The infographic is to tell students about Digital Literacy. We want to show ideas in a way that is easy to look at and understand.

---

## Objective

We want students to know these things from the infographic:

- What Digital Literacy is

- Why Digital Literacy tools are important

- How to be safe on the internet

- How to look professional online

- How to write good emails

---

##  Tool Used

- We used Canva to make the infographic

---

##  Key Topics Covered

### 1. What is Digital Literacy?

Digital Literacy is about using Digital Literacy tools in a way. It means you can find things online check if they are true and talk to people online.

### 2. Useful Digital Literacy Tools for Students

- Google Drive is for storing and sharing files

- GitHub is for showing coding projects

- Canva is for making designs and presentations

- ChatGPT is, for learning and solving problems

### 3. Safe Internet Practices

- Do not share your passwords or special codes

- Use passwords

- Do not click on bad links or open bad emails

- Turn on two-factor authentication

### 4. Professional Online Presence

- Use your name and make a professional profile

- Keep your LinkedIn page updated

- Do not put bad things online

- Make a portfolio to show your work

### 5. Email Etiquette

- Use a clear subject line

- Start your email with a greeting
